# Better Level Thumbnails - Measurement

Here I am providing how the mod Measures information visually.

## Thumbnail Points
<img width="128" height="128" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d3d012e5-fbf0-4cf4-b0c9-f1a3e0878927" />

Thumbnail Points is the way the Better Thumbnails mod measures your active thumbnails. _**This measurement doesnt count for replaced thumbnails**._

## Dashboard information

The mod displays the following information from the dashboard:

### _->_ Local (your stats)
- Acceptance Rate
- Active Thumbnails
- Uploads
- Accepted thumbnails from uploaded
- Thumbnails accepted that are not replacments (unique)
- Rejected thumbnails
- Pending submissions
- Thumbnails you submitted first, but that got replaced
- Cool bar displaying your acceptance rate

### _->_ Global (information from the server)
- Everything from the local stats, translated to total from all users (except for acceptance rate and the cool bar)
- Total users
- Monthly users
- Storage used (Measured in **GB**, not to confuse with ***GiB***)
